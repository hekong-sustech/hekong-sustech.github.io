Join Us!

We are constantly looking for dedicated and talented people to join our lab at the levels of undergraduate interns/visitors, Master students, PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and research-track professors.

For postdoctoral or higher level positions: the candidate should have obtained or is expecting to obtain a PhD degree in control, robotics, mechatronics, machine learning, and other related fields. The candidate should have published 1-2 papers in top journals or conferences.

For Master/PhD student positions: the candidate is expected to have very strong skills in at least one of the following areas: programming (Python, PyTorch, C, etc); embedded systems development; ROS; Mechatronics & hardware design. Experience in major competitions such as RoboMaster will be a plus.

For undergraduate interns/visitors: the candidate needs to have or is in the process of acquiring programming skills (Python, C). Undergraduate students from SUSTech or based in Shenzhen/the Greater Bay area are specially welcomed.

Note to SUSTech undergraduate students: What we offer includes, but not limited to the following:

  • lab facilities

    • more than 30+ motion capture systems

    • Unitree humanoid robots, robot dog; wheeled mobile robots

    • Other platforms developed in-house, including Cubli, acoustic perception systems, etc

  • The team's supervision, and an ad hoc but somewhat systematic research training experience

  • Credit-based recognition

  • Recommendation letter from the lab director

南科大在校本科生，在联系之前，请认真阅读。我们可以提供:

  • 先进的实验平台和科研条件

  • 得到指导老师和课题组高年级学生的指导，获得较为系统的科学训练，迅速加强相关技能，尝试和探索自己感兴趣的方向

  • 所参与的项目，如有论文/专利产出，给与基于贡献的承认和作者排序

  • 指导老师推荐信（仅对在课题组做了实质性工作的同学）

Should you have further questions, feel free to contact us.
